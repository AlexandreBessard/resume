BTS Services Informatiques aux Organisations, Option Logicielles et Applications Metiers (SIO SLAM)

Click on the diploma to zoom in!

The BTS SIO (Information Technology Services) degree with a specialization in SLAM (Software Solutions and Business Applications) is a diploma from the French higher education system that prepares students to perform functions in the field of information technology within organizations. This program aims to train professionals capable of designing, developing, and maintaining software solutions that meet the specific needs of businesses.

The BTS SIO SLAM option focuses particularly on the development of business applications, meaning software designed to address the specific needs of a company or organization. Graduates of this program are equipped to create tailor-made applications to automate processes, manage data, or facilitate specific professional activities.

BTS Management des Unités Commerciales (MUC)

Click on the diploma to zoom in!

The BTS Management des Unités Commerciales (Business Unit Management) is a diploma from the French higher education system that prepares students for roles in managing commercial units within organizations. This program aims to train professionals capable of overseeing and optimizing business activities, ensuring customer satisfaction, and contributing to the overall success of the organization.

The BTS MUC option focuses particularly on developing skills related to commercial management, sales, customer relationship management, and marketing. Graduates of this program are equipped to handle various aspects of running a business unit, from sales strategies to customer service excellence.
